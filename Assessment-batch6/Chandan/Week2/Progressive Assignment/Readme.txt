1)     Roster Restaurant Tracker  (application to track the Restaurant services )

Created a roster restaurant application. Named it FunnyGrill. 

localhost/FunnyGrill/Welcome is the Welcome page

On welcome page a list of restaurants are displayed

Customer can Sign up or log in from here.

Upon logging in a customer can select a restaurant from welcome page and either book a table at that restaurant or place a parcel order.

For booking a table the customer needs to provide a date and select a time slot from a dropdown.

for ordering a parcel the customer needs to select foods from the available menu at that restaurant and quantities of each food item.

after booking a table or ordering a parcel, the customer can go to the tracking tab to check the status of their bookings or orders.

Customers can view their profile in the profile section and can also log out when they are done.
